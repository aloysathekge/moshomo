# Competitive analysis — LeavePro

Source: https://www.leavepro.co.za/features/ (reviewed 2026-06-27)

LeavePro is a **SA-focused, leave-only** product — the closest direct comparison
for Moshomo's Leave module.

## Where Moshomo already matches
- Apply for leave, approvals with context (balances + who's away), team calendar, history
- Working-day counting, SA public holidays + custom company holidays, half-days
- Document storage, company branding/logo, RLS audit trail, unlimited admins
- Overlap + balance enforcement (we actually block these; they don't advertise it)

## Gaps — what LeavePro has that we don't (ranked by SA-market weight)
1. **Notifications** (table stakes) — email on submit/approve/decline, manager reminders,
   SMS, "who's away" daily/weekly digests, birthday notifications. *We have none.* (Deferred by user.)
2. **Accruals + BCEA compliance** — auto-accrue monthly/weekly/bi-weekly; built-in BCEA
   rules per leave type; SA sick-leave 6-month/36-month cycle + expiry; long-service
   auto-increase; forfeiting (manual + auto + "use it or lose it" warnings). *Our allowances
   are static numbers — biggest functional gap.*
3. **More leave types + custom types** — annual, sick, family, maternity, parental, study,
   long-service, admin-defined custom. *We have 4 (annual/sick/family/unpaid).* **← building next.**
4. **Reporting & export** — standard reports, Excel export, scheduled month-end emails,
   sick-leave abuse detection, unused/forfeit reports. *We have none.*
5. **Admin power tools** — delegate (apply on behalf), bulk apply (annual shutdown),
   block dates (peak periods), department-level approver routing.
6. **Integrations** — Outlook/Google calendar sync, SSO (M365/Google Workspace).

## Where Moshomo wins
- **AI-native** — Moshomo AI answers + proposes leave decisions. LeavePro has nothing like it.
- **Beyond leave** — Shifts + a modular à-la-carte platform vs a single leave app.

## Pricing
LeavePro: tiered per-employee, no long-term contract, 14-day free trial (no card), free setup.
A "Plus" tier gates the advanced items (SMS, auto-forfeit, scheduled reports, SSO, block dates, etc.).

## Recommended priorities (to be competitive on Leave)
1. Notifications (deferred). 2. Accrual/BCEA engine (the moat). 3. Expand leave types (now).
4. Reporting + Excel export.
