# Competitive analysis — Sage HR (Sage Business Cloud HR, ZA)

Sources (reviewed 2026-06-27):
- https://www.sage.com/en-za/sage-business-cloud/hr/
- https://www.sage.com/en-za/sage-business-cloud/hr/features/leave-management/
- https://www.sage.com/en-za/sage-business-cloud/hr/features/app/
- https://www.sage.com/en-za/human-resources/performance-management/
- https://www.sage.com/en-za/news/press-releases/2025/10/ (next-gen HR+Payroll launch)

Sage is a different weight class: an established **full HR + payroll suite** for SA
SMEs→mid-market, sold as **modular add-ons**, anchored by SA-compliant payroll.

## Modules / features
- Core HR (records, org charts)
- Self-service (ESS/MSS) via native iOS/Android app — details, leave, payslips, expenses, schedules
- Leave management (request/approve/track; BCEA accruals)
- Performance management (goals, OKRs, reviews)
- Recruitment & onboarding (Advanced tier)
- Timesheets / time & attendance, expense claims, benefits admin
- Reporting/analytics, integrated SA payroll

## The big strategic signal
**Sage sells HR as à-la-carte modules priced per employee** — exactly Moshomo's
entitlement model. The market leader validates the pricing architecture.

## Where Sage beats us (mostly out of our V1 scope)
- SA-compliant **payroll** (their anchor; V1 excludes payroll)
- **Performance management** (OKRs/reviews), **recruitment/onboarding**, expenses/benefits/timesheets
- Mature **native mobile app** (ours is an Expo shell)
- Brand trust, scale, compliance depth

## Where Moshomo differentiates (the wedge)
- **AI-native** agentic assistant (answers + proposes actions). Sage has bolt-on AI, not this.
- Modern, fast, opinionated UX vs Sage's enterprise heaviness
- Smart Shifts with AI + modular affordability aimed at SMEs Sage underserves

## Implications for the roadmap
1. Don't chase Sage on breadth (payroll/recruitment/performance) in V1 — compete on
   **AI-native + modern UX + SME price**, with our modules best-in-class.
2. The à-la-carte entitlement model is validated — keep building it.
3. The Leave **accrual/BCEA engine** is the SA compliance baseline (both Sage & LeavePro have it).
4. Mobile is a real long-term gap vs Sage — the native app must become more than a shell.

Positioning: *"the AI-native, modular workforce OS for SA SMEs."*
