Work-cycle rule:
- Before meaningful work, read .agent/progress/current.md.
- After meaningful work, update .agent/progress/current.md if the next session should know what changed.
- When work is complete or paused after a large change, create a handoff in .agent/progress/handoffs/.
- Update AGENTS.md only when stable onboarding information changes, such as architecture, source of truth, verification, approval gates, commands, rules, skills, or adapters.
- Do not finish meaningful work without checking whether progress, handoff, or AGENTS.md needs an update.

Trae should use this as a pointer to the canonical AGENTS.md and .agent context.
Trae must follow the work-cycle rule above before ending meaningful work.
