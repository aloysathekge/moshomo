# Moshomo V1 PRD

## Product

Moshomo

## Tagline

AI-Native Workforce Operating System

---

# Vision

Moshomo helps businesses manage employees, leave, and shift scheduling through modern workflows powered by AI.

Unlike traditional HR systems that simply store information, Moshomo actively assists managers by automating workforce operations and providing intelligent recommendations through Pori.

The first version focuses on solving workforce management before payroll.

---

# Problem

Businesses spend too much time:

* Managing employee records
* Tracking leave requests
* Building schedules
* Finding replacement staff
* Answering repetitive employee questions

Most companies still rely on spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected systems.

---

# Solution

Moshomo provides:

1. Employee Management
2. Leave Management
3. Smart Shift Scheduling
4. Workforce AI Assistant (Pori)

Payroll is intentionally excluded from V1.

# Platforms

## Web

Available to:

* Admins
* Managers
* Employees

The web experience exposes the workflows permitted by the user's company role.

## Mobile

Available to:

* Admins
* Managers
* Employees

Built using Expo React Native.

The mobile experience exposes the same role-permitted capabilities as web, adapted for mobile interaction.

## Platform Access Rule

Permissions are determined by company membership and role, not by platform.

* Admins can perform their permitted administrative work on web and mobile.
* Managers can perform their permitted management work on web and mobile.
* Employees can perform their permitted self-service work on web and mobile.
* Navigation and presentation may differ by device, but authorization and available business capabilities must remain consistent.

Pori may automatically approve leave if:

* Employee has sufficient balance
* Team coverage remains healthy

# AI Features

## Schedule Generation

Manager Prompt:

"Generate next week's schedule."

Pori considers:

* Existing leave
* Employee availability
* Historical assignments
* Fair distribution

---

## Shift Gap Detection

When shifts become unfilled:

Pori detects:

* Vacant shifts
* Understaffed periods

---

## Replacement Suggestions

Pori identifies suitable employees based on:

* Availability
* Skills
* Overtime exposure
* Fairness
